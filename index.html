Welcome to the ever morphing Autism Awareness HUB!!!

Welcome to Autism Hub. I'm Jason, this is Ian and his mom Sharon, we are an autism family or rather a family that deals with autism daily. The good, the bad & the ugly. And also, the insanely beautiful and magnificent!
It is the intent of this site to gather as many helpful resources and educational as well as communiy opportunities in order to enable our son and others to thrive and grow through learning and connecting with each other.
First I will start by saying that this is in no way to take away from anyone else, it is only my attempt to be part of a solution based society and as a father figure to a wonderful young man, I want so much to be of value and help with anything I can. So here I go.
I will be building and fleshing out this site and making changes as I gather up more resources. This will be an ongoing process and I don't suppose that will ever cease to be the case if I am able to see this through..

Our Mission

To give ease of acces to Educational Programs, Social Events, Conventions, Awareness anything you can think of
We want to try and help. Because we can.
